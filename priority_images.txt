# Priority Image Downloads - Original 3 Homes

Start with these 3 homes since they're the most famous and well-documented:

## 1. Kaufmann Residence (Easiest - Most Photos Available)

### Where to Find:
- **Wikipedia:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kaufmann_Desert_House
- **Wikimedia Commons:** Search "Kaufmann Desert House"
- **ArchDaily:** Many professional photos
- **Slim Aarons:** "Poolside Gossip" photo is iconic

### Image Types to Get:
1. Exterior with pool (the famous shot)
2. Slim Aarons "Poolside Gossip" photo
3. Living room
4. Kitchen
5. Master bedroom
6. Pool/patio area
7. Entry/eaves detail
8. Night shots
9. Historical photos
10. Recent restoration photos

### Filenames:
Save as: `kaufmann-1.jpg` through `kaufmann-10.jpg`

---

## 2. Palm Springs Visitor Center (Very Easy - Public Building)

### Where to Find:

- **Instagram:** #palmspringsvisitorcenter

### Image Types to Get:
1. Hyperbolic paraboloid roof exterior
2. Side view showing roof shape
3. Interior visitor center
4. Entrance
5. Historical gas station photos
6. Night lighting
7. Detail of corrugated steel
8. Context shot with mountains
9. People for scale
10. Adjacent Shell station (Cody)

### Filenames:
Save as: `visitor-center-1.jpg` through `visitor-center-10.jpg`

## 3. Swiss Miss Houses (Easy - Instagram Famous)

### Where to Find:
- **Instagram:** #palmspringsswissmiss #swissmisshouse
- **Real Estate:** Zillow/Redfin listings (they're popular vacation rentals)
- **Palm Springs Modern Committee:** Archives
- **Airbnb/VRBO:** Rental listings have many photos

### Image Types to Get:
1. A-frame entryway exterior
2. Street view with multiple houses
3. Interior with peaked ceiling
4. Kitchen (mid-century cabinets)
5. Living room
6. Pool/patio
7. Bedroom with A-frame detail
8. Close-up of Swiss Miss design
9. Landscaping/palm trees
10. Evening/twilight shots

### Filenames:
Save as: `swiss-miss-1.jpg` through `swiss-miss-10.jpg`

## Quick Download Tips

### Method 1: Browser
1. Right-click image → "Save Image As"
2. Rename immediately to correct filename
3. Keep track of which number you're on

### Method 2: Bulk Download
1. Collect URLs in a text file (one per line)
2. Save as `kaufmann_urls.txt`
3. Run: `./download_images.sh kaufmann_urls.txt kaufmann`
4. Script auto-numbers 1-10

### Quality Check:
- [ ] Image is at least 1200px wide
- [ ] File size under 500KB
- [ ] Good lighting, not blurry
- [ ] Shows the home prominently
- [ ] No heavy watermarks

### After Downloading:
Test in the app:
```bash
cd ~/clawd/tools/palm-springs-tour
python3 -m http.server 8000
# Open http://localhost:8000
# Click on each home to verify images load
```
